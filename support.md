## Common questions

**Is my data backed up?**
Yes — your readings, medicine profiles, and dosage logs sync automatically to your account and are available on any device you sign in on.

**How do I delete my data?**
Go to **Settings → Delete All Data & Account**. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

**How do I export my data for my doctor?**
Use the **Export** tab for CSV/PDF exports over a custom date range, or tap **Share** on the **Charts** screen to share a PDF report for whatever time range you're currently viewing.
