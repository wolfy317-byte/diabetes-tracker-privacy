# Privacy Policy — Glimpse

**Last updated:** July 16, 2026

Glimpse ("the app") is developed by Allen Gresham ("we," "us"). This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and how you can control it.

## Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google or Apple, your name (if you choose to share it). This is used solely to authenticate you and identify your data.

**Health tracking data.** The app stores the blood glucose readings, medicine profiles, and dosage logs you enter, along with the personal target-range settings you configure. This data is entered by you and used only to power the app's own tracking, charting, and export features.

We do not collect any data beyond what's described above. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

## How Your Data Is Stored and Used

Your account and health tracking data are stored in a Supabase-hosted database and are synced across your own signed-in devices so your history is available wherever you use the app. Your data is used exclusively to provide the app's features to you — it is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used to build a profile for marketing purposes.

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** provides authentication and database hosting for the app. See [Supabase's privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for how they handle data on our behalf.
- **Google** and **Apple** sign-in are optional ways to create an account. If you use them, we receive only the basic profile information (email, name) those providers share with us for authentication.

## Your Controls

- You can sign out of the app at any time from Settings.
- You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from **Settings → Delete All Data & Account**. This immediately and irreversibly removes your readings, medicine profiles, dosage logs, and settings from our database.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. Deleting your account (as described above) permanently removes your data from our systems.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above.
